# Code of Conduct

Ridge follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

By participating in this project, you agree to abide by its terms.

## Enforcement

Concerns about behavior in project spaces may be reported to the maintainers at
**jaavila.dev@outlook.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly. The maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and
security of the reporter of any incident.

For the full text of the Code of Conduct, see the linked document above.
